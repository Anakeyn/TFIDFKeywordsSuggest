Anakeyn TF-IDF Keywords Suggest

Anakeyn TF-IDF Keywords Suggest is a keywords suggestion tool.
This tool searches in the first x pages responding to a given keyword in Google. Next the system will get the content of the pages in order to find popular and original keywords in the subject area. The system works with a TF-IDF algorithm.
